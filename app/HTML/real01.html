About the Bookworn Bookshop

Since 1993,The Bookworm Bookshop has offered rare and hard-to-find titles for thediscerning reader. The Bookworm offers:

  • Friendly, knowledgeable, and courteous help
  • Free coffee and juice for our customers
  • A well-lit reading room so you can "tyr before you buy"
  • Four friendly cats: Esmerelda, Catherine, Dulcinea and Beatrice

Our hours are 10am to 9pm weekdays, noon to 7 on weekends.
